== Design decisions ==
- **Aesthetic direction**: Editorial magazine meets casual coffee-shop learning - sophisticated but approachable
- **Typography**: Fraunces for display (distinctive serif with character) + DM Sans for body (clean, readable)
- **Color palette**: Warm terracotta/burnt orange to feel inviting and energetic, completely avoiding cold blues
- **Layout philosophy**: Asymmetric elements, generous whitespace, overlapping decorative elements, confident use of scale
- **Component design**: Rounded-3xl corners everywhere, 2xl borders for emphasis, layered shadows, gradient accents
- **Interaction design**: Hover scale effects, animated arrows, smooth transitions, visual feedback on all interactive elements
- **Mobile-first**: Responsive breakpoints throughout, stacking layouts, touch-friendly button sizes
